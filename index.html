  1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your Heltec V4 device
  2. Open this page in Chrome or Edge browser (required for Web Bluetooth)
  3. Click the "BLE (Bluetooth)" tab below
  4. Click "Scan for Devices" button
  5. Select your Heltec V4 from the list
  6. Wait for configuration sync (monitor in Logs tab)
  7. Navigate to Dashboard to view device information
